# PermitPilot — Demo Script

**Runtime: ~75 seconds. Rehearse once out loud before showing.**

---

## Setup Before You Start

- Dashboard open and loaded
- One case pre-staged in **Corrections Required** (shows the correction flow without creating it live)
- One case pre-staged in **New** with 0 documents (shows the full intake flow)
- Optional: homeowner upload link open in an incognito tab, ready to show

---

## The Script

**[Dashboard]**

"This is the PermitPilot dashboard — our permit pipeline at a glance. Active cases, what needs attention today, what was recently approved. The flagged cases here have been sitting more than five days with no activity and we own the next move. Not a full inbox — just what's actually blocked right now."

---

**[Click New Case → fill in quickly]**

"Creating a case takes about 30 seconds. Address, homeowner contact info, pick the county —"

*[select county from dropdown]*

"— and the document checklist populates automatically from the jurisdiction. We don't build that list — it's pulled from the county requirements."

---

**[Click to Documents tab]**

"Seven required documents. Nothing received yet. Instead of calling or texting the homeowner ourselves, we hit *Request All Missing* — one SMS goes out with a secure upload link."

*[click Request All Missing]*

"They get a link on their phone. No app, no account. Tap, pick the file, done."

---

**[Open incognito tab with homeowner upload link — or just describe it]**

"This is what they see. Plain page, works on any phone. When they hit submit, the document lands directly on the case."

---

**[Back in the office view — show a doc marked Received]**

"Checklist updates immediately. Progress bar moves. When all required docs are in, we advance the status —"

*[advance to Submitted, enter a permit number in the field]*

"— record the permit tracking number right here, and the case is Submitted. Everything in one place, one source of truth."

---

**[Switch to the pre-staged Corrections Required case]**

"The permit office came back with a correction. We paste their exact wording in — nothing gets lost in a text thread. Status auto-advances to Corrections Required, so it surfaces on the dashboard."

*[show one correction, click Resolve]*

"Once it's resolved, the case tells us the next step: advance to Resubmitted."

---

**[Cases list — quick cut]**

"Every case in the pipeline. Search by address or homeowner. Filter by status. The short reference code under each address is what you read to a homeowner when they call asking for an update."

---

**That's it. Open, collect, submit, correct, approve.**

---

## Handling Common Questions

**"What does the homeowner see?"**
> "A text message with a link. They tap it, upload the file from their phone — no login, no app. Takes about 45 seconds. Works on Android and iPhone."

**"What if we already have the documents in email?"**
> "Office staff can upload directly. The homeowner flow is for when you need to chase a document — it saves the back-and-forth."

**"What's not built yet?"**
> "Right now it's Florida roofing only. Email notifications are on the roadmap. Multi-trade — HVAC, electrical — is next after that. Adding a new county is a one-row data change, no code."

**"How does it handle the permit number?"**
> "You record it when you advance to Submitted. It shows on the case detail and in the sidebar for anyone who pulls up the case later."
